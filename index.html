Supercharge your trading.

AstroTools is a DeFi dashboard that bridges the information gap between centralized and decentralized exchanges, giving you an analytical edge to supercharge your trading experience.

Reduce clutter with an all-in-one interface.

Buy or sell Uniswap tokens directly within AstroTools without leaving the site. Search and view a token's chart and trade history as well add them a favorites list with price alerts.

View transactions in real-time.

No intentional delays in price and trading data. Transaction history and chart candles are delivered in real-time directly from the Uniswap API. Deep historical data means chart candles can be loaded for as far as transactions have been first recorded.

Filter out potential scams at a glance.

Search results are prioritized by age, liquidity, and trading volume, with easy links to third party resources, to combat duplicative scam coins. New listing explorer highlights liquidity pulls as suspected scams.

Membership Passes

Membership Passes are activated by holding sufficient ASTRO tokens that automatically unlock advanced toolsets in cryptocurrency and DeFi trading once connected with MetaMask. Membership slots are hard capped to provide experienced traders with a competitive edge. Cancel anytime by transferring your membership back through Uniswap.
